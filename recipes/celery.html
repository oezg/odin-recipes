Celery

Description

This is a delicious meal for anyone who loves vegetables and celery in particular. It is also relatively easy to cook. You can cook the rice or potatoes the way you like them. I prefer to eat celery with potatoes.

You can serve the celery with vegetables. My favorite vegetable to serve with celery is carrots. The orange color of carrot and the white color of the celery create a colorful dish. Combined with the yellow color of potatoes, this dish is not only tasty but also full of colors.

Ingredients

  • 1 celery
  • 2 carrots
  • 300g potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • grape seed oil

Steps

  1. Cut the onion, carrots and potatoes in small pieces
  2. Cook the onion and then the carrot and then the potatoes in a pan
  3. Heat some grape seed oil in a pan and then fry the celery in slices
  4. Fry the celery slices for five minutes on one side and three more minutes on the other side
  5. Serve the celery, the carrots and the potatoes with a slice of orange
